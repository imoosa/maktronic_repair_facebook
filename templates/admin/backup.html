Backup & Restore

Download a complete copy of your database and uploaded files,
or restore from a previous backup ZIP.

⬇️
Download Backup
Creates a .zip file containing your entire database and all uploaded invoice files — ready to save on your PC or upload anywhere.
What's included:
✅  Full SQLite database (jobs, users, logs, notifications)
✅  All uploaded invoices & photos
✅  Timestamped filename for easy versioning
⬇️ Download Backup ZIP
  • Save to your Desktop or a USB drive for safekeeping
  • Upload to Google Drive / Dropbox for cloud storage
  • Make a backup before every major update
⬆️
Restore from Backup
Upload a previously downloaded .zip backup to restore your database and files. Your current data will be replaced.

⚠️ Warning

Restoring will overwrite the current database. A safety copy of your existing DB is kept automatically before restore.

📂
Click to select backup .zip file
  • Only use backups from this same app
  • The app auto-saves a .bak copy before overwriting
  • Refresh the page after restore to see updated data
ℹ️ Backup Tips
📅 How often?
Take a backup weekly at minimum, and always before adding new users or making bulk changes.
☁️ Google Drive
Download the ZIP, then drag it into your Google Drive folder or use the Drive web app to upload.
🔒 Keep it safe
The ZIP contains customer data. Store it in a private folder, not a shared drive.
♻️ After restore
Log out and log back in after restoring to ensure session data is fresh.
